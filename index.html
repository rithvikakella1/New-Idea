How it works

From clinical note to code in three steps

Our AI reads clinical language the way a coder would — but 100× faster, with no fatigue.

01

Dictate or paste your note

Speak directly into the app using speech-to-text, or paste any free-text clinical note, discharge summary, or SOAP note. No special formatting required.

02

AI extracts & maps codes

Our model identifies diagnoses, procedures, and services — then maps each to the correct ICD-10 or CPT code.

03

Review & export

Review the AI-generated codes, make edits if needed, and export directly to your billing system.

Features

Everything your billing team needs

Powerful AI tools built specifically for the complexity of medical coding.

ICD-10 & CPT Extraction

Accurately pulls both diagnosis codes (ICD-10-CM) and procedure codes (CPT) from unstructured clinical text.

Confidence Scoring

Each extracted code comes with a confidence score so coders can prioritize their review where it matters most.

Compliance Guardrails

Built-in payer rules and NCCI edits flag potentially unbillable code combinations before submission.

Real-Time Processing

Results appear in under 3 seconds — fast enough to keep pace with a busy clinical workflow.

EHR-Agnostic

Works with notes from any EHR — Epic, Cerner, Athena, or plain-text exports. No integration required to get started.

Team Collaboration

Share extractions with your coding team, track edits, and maintain an audit trail for every note processed.
